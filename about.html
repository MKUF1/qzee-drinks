Why he started Qzee

Althaf Sanjari wanted a brand with better taste, better energy, and a more exciting identity than ordinary juice shelves.

His goal was to create something customers enjoy seeing, tasting, and coming back to, with a natural fruit feel and a more memorable brand mood.

He shaped Qzee around freshness, colorful packaging energy, and a product story that feels fun without losing trust.
