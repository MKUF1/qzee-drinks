About the brand

Qzee was imagined as a fruit brand people could trust at first sight and first sip.

The vision is simple: bring together organic fruits, a naturally refreshing taste, and a premium identity that feels polished without losing honesty.

Founder

Althaf Sanjari wanted to build a better feeling around juice.

Vision

He believed juice should feel cleaner, more natural, and more premium than what most people usually find in the market.

Why he started Qzee

Althaf Sanjari saw an opportunity to create a fruit brand that could communicate freshness, quality, and trust more clearly through both product and presentation.

Instead of building around noise, he wanted a brand that feels calm, natural, and memorable, especially for customers who care about taste and ingredient quality.

What he wanted it to represent

Qzee was shaped to represent organic fruit goodness, refined refreshment, and a modern premium look that still feels welcoming and accessible.

That is why the brand leans into natural taste, fresh visuals, and a more elevated customer experience across every touchpoint.

What makes us different

Qzee is built to feel fresh, not artificial.

The difference is not just in the juice profile. It is also in the way the brand looks, speaks, and earns confidence.
