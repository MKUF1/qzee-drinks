Our Story

Tradition, purity, and innovation in every bottle.

Made to feel real

Qzee is born from the heart of Malnad Beverages, Bengaluru, with a simple goal to bring people closer to natural taste.

Our drinks are made without preservatives, artificial shortcuts, or compromise, so every sip feels pure, fresh, and honest.

What we offer

Qzee is made to stay affordable, refreshing, and easy to enjoy for everyone, anywhere.

We offer thirteen core flavours: four bold soda-based mixes and eight naturally extracted variants for a cleaner fruit-forward taste.

Every bottle follows 100% hygienic standards and reaches customers through Distributors across Karnataka, Kerala, and Tamil Nadu.
